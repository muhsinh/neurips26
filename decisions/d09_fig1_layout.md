# Decision 09: Figure 1 (thesis schematic) layout

**Time:** 2026-05-04 14:35 PT
**Choice faced:** Visual structure of the thesis-schematic figure.
**Alternatives considered:**
- A: Three columns showing failure modes converging via arrows to "distribution shift current fusion architectures cannot bridge," with a small inset showing the proposed cross-modal generative prior.
